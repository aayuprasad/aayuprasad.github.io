Dal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (dal)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions:

  1. Wash the lentils and soak them in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Saute until onions turn golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add soaked lentils and water. Pressure cook for 2-3 whistles.
  6. Once pressure releases, open the cooker, and mash the dal lightly. Adjust consistency if required.
  7. Serve hot with rice.

Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, add rice, water, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and rice is cooked.
  4. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.

Aloo Bharta

Ingredients:

  • 3 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon mustard oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onion and green chilies. Saute until onions turn translucent.
  3. Add turmeric powder and mashed potatoes. Mix well.
  4. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are heated through.
  5. Adjust salt to taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
